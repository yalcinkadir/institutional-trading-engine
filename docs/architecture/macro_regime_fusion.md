# Macro Regime Fusion

## Purpose

The Macro Regime Fusion layer combines multiple institutional intelligence systems into a unified macro-level portfolio context.

It integrates:

- Market Regime Intelligence
- Cross-Asset Intelligence
- Tail Risk Intelligence
- Liquidity Intelligence
- Volatility Context

The goal is to improve portfolio-level positioning and institutional macro awareness.

## Outputs

The engine produces:

- Macro Score
- Macro Regime
- Portfolio Bias
- Explainable Reasoning

## Regime States

| Regime | Meaning |
|---|---|
| macro_risk_on | Strong offensive macro environment |
| macro_constructive | Favorable but selective environment |
| macro_uncertain | Mixed macro conditions |
| macro_risk_off | Defensive / capital preservation environment |

## Institutional Importance

Institutional systems rarely rely on a single market signal.

Macro fusion improves:

- capital allocation
- exposure management
- portfolio defense
- conviction calibration
- macro-aware execution

## Operational Notes

- deterministic scoring
- bounded outputs
- explainable logic
- resistant to isolated signal failures
- designed for portfolio-level intelligence
