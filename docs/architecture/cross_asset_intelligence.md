# Cross-Asset Intelligence

## Purpose

The Cross-Asset Intelligence layer evaluates macro alignment across multiple asset classes.

Institutional systems rarely analyze equities in isolation.

Cross-asset relationships often provide early warning signals for:

- risk regime changes
- liquidity stress
- macro transitions
- volatility expansion

## Integrated Asset Classes

- Equities
- Bonds
- US Dollar
- Gold
- Volatility

## Outputs

The engine generates:

- Cross-Asset Score
- Regime Alignment
- Risk Bias
- Human-readable Reasoning

## Regime States

| Regime | Meaning |
|---|---|
| risk_on | Equities dominate defensive assets |
| mixed | No clear macro alignment |
| risk_off | Defensive assets outperform |

## Institutional Importance

Cross-asset analysis improves:

- macro awareness
- regime detection
- portfolio defense
- liquidity anticipation
- probabilistic decision quality

## Operational Notes

- deterministic scoring
- bounded outputs
- explainable logic
- macro-aware architecture
- no black-box forecasting
