# Multi-Factor Fusion Engine

## Purpose

The Multi-Factor Fusion Engine combines multiple institutional intelligence layers into a single explainable decision score.

This reduces isolated signal dependency and improves institutional robustness.

## Integrated Layers

- Regime Intelligence
- Tail Risk Intelligence
- Liquidity Intelligence
- Feature Attribution
- Portfolio Risk Intelligence
- Execution Confidence

## Outputs

The engine produces:

- Fusion Score
- Confidence Score
- Conviction Classification
- Human-Readable Reasoning

## Classification Levels

| Classification | Meaning |
|---|---|
| high_conviction | Strong alignment across factors |
| moderate_conviction | Favorable but mixed environment |
| low_conviction | Uncertain conditions |
| avoid | Risk dominates opportunity |

## Institutional Importance

Institutional systems rarely rely on isolated indicators.

The fusion layer creates:

- contextual intelligence
- probabilistic alignment
- explainable signal fusion
- adaptive institutional reasoning

## Operational Notes

- deterministic logic
- bounded confidence
- explainable outputs
- no black-box optimization
- resilient against single-factor degradation
