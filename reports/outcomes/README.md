# Outcome Tracking

This directory stores outcome-tracking data for institutional reports.

Purpose:
- validate signal quality
- evaluate historical recommendations
- measure setup performance
- compare market regimes
- support adaptive intelligence
- improve long-term decision quality

---

## Planned Metrics

### Signal Performance

Examples:
- breakout success rate
- pullback success rate
- trend continuation quality
- failed setup frequency

---

### Regime Analysis

Examples:
- bull market performance
- bear market performance
- high volatility behavior
- low volatility behavior

---

### Risk Metrics

Examples:
- drawdown tracking
- stop-loss frequency
- volatility-adjusted returns
- correlation exposure

---

### Portfolio Analytics

Examples:
- sector concentration
- relative strength persistence
- leadership rotation
- weak-name persistence

---
